# Solicitor Enquiry — Template Message

Copy-paste for contact forms or initial emails. Adjust firm name / specific details as needed.

## Email preamble (for firms without a contact form)

Use this block at the top of an email, followed by the message template below.

```
Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to enquire about your family law services. My details are as follows:

Name: Rupert Thomas
Email: rupert@pebmarsh.com
Telephone: 07740 706315
Address: 19 Westgate Street, Long Melford, Suffolk, CO10 9DS
Enquiry: Family & Relationships — Divorce and Consent Order
```

## Message (for forms or after the preamble above)

---

I am seeking representation for a divorce and financial consent order. My wife and I have been married for 27 years (married August 1998). We have three adult children — no child arrangement issues.

I have moved out of the marital home and am currently renting. My wife remains at the property. She has already instructed a solicitor.

The key issues are:

- Sale of the marital home (detached property in Pebmarsh, valued at £550k–£650k with an active subsidence insurance claim — liability accepted)
- RBS offset mortgage of ~£310k at 7.10%
- Joint debts of ~£25k to be decoupled
- Multiple pensions on both sides requiring valuation (one with an urgent retirement date of 19 June 2026)
- Significant income disparity — I am seeking a clean break with no ongoing spousal maintenance

To help me decide on representation, it would be useful to know:

- Your experience with pension sharing orders and CETVs, and whether you can handle the consent order end-to-end including the pension sharing annex
- A fee estimate for a consent order with pension sharing (assuming agreement is reached), and whether you offer fixed or capped fees
- Who would handle my case day-to-day

I have a detailed briefing pack prepared with all financial figures, correspondence, and supporting documents ready to share at an initial consultation.
