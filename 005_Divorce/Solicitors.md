Budget: £3,000–£6,000 for a straightforward consent order. Resolution accreditation preferred.

## Top Picks

### Ellisons
Colchester / Bury St Edmunds / Ipswich / Chelmsford. Lisa Dawson (Partner, Head of Family) + large team. 4.9/5 (1,510 reviews). Resolution accredited. **Free exploratory call**, then fixed-fee 1hr meeting. Best-resourced local option.

### Greene & Greene
Bury St Edmunds. Stuart Hughes (Partner, Legal 500 "Leading Partner"). 4.6/5 (145 reviews). Chambers UK 2026 top tier. Premium local option — "exemplary lawyer" per Legal 500.

### Atkins Dellow
Sudbury / Bury St Edmunds / London. Alan Caldwell (36 years). 4.9/5 (1,312 reviews). Closest to Long Melford. Published pension/divorce guidance. Free phone chat.

### Fullers Family Law
Bury St Edmunds. 4.9/5 (547 reviews). Family-law-only firm. Has "Together We Part" one-lawyer option — could save costs if Heather agrees. Free first call.
